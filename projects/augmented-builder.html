Augmented Builder

Technologies: Unity, ARCore / AR Foundation, C#

Vision Statement

"Augmented Builder" is an AR city-builder where players place workers—lumberjacks, builders—on a 10×10 grid to harvest resources and construct a growing city in real space.

Gameplay Synopsis

  • Initialize a blank tree-populated map and deploy lumberjacks to clear resources.
  • Use collected wood to build roads, housing, and entertainment buildings via on-screen UI touch controls.
  • Assign workers to specialized roles (builder, farmer, logger) and manage the time to meet their needs.

Controls

All interactions are touch-based: tap cells to create tasks or construct structures.

Interface

The bottom HUD shows worker counts and resource inventory; the top displays city population, job and entertainment needs. Main menu offers New Game, Continue, and Settings.
