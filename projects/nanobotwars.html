Nanobot Wars

Period: Nov 2021– Jen 2022

Technologies: Unity, Photon Networking

Vision Statement

“Nanobot Wars” is a strategy simulation where players design and deploy swarms of nanobots to battle enemy creations in an arena, using element placement and upgrades to influence the outcome.

Gameplay Synopsis

  • Lab Customization: Select and configure your nanobot’s hardware (legs, sensors, weapons) and software behaviors before deployment.
  • Pre-Battle Setup: Place energy sources, defensive walls, or traps on your side of the arena.
  • Battle Phase: Reveal the full battlefield and watch a 45-second automated engagement where nanobots gather energy, replicate, and clash until one swarm prevails.

Game Modes

Single Player: Battle AI scientists to earn parts and master mechanics. Tutorial levels introduce core gameplay.
VS Mode: Head-to-head online matches where both players configure their swarm and tools before automated battles.
