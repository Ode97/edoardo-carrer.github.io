Synth & Sinners

Period: Apr – Jun 2021

Technologies: Unity, Python, Tobii Eye Tracker 5, Empatica Embrace Plus

Objectives

Develop a working prototype featuring a single cooperative online map where teams of three players collaborate in real time to overcome dynamic challenges.

The full Game Design Document (GDD) extends this vision to multiple interconnected maps, advanced progression systems, in-game voice chat and deep cooperative mechanics.

Overview

In this online co-op prototype, groups of three form squads to tackle short, intense synth-wave-style Police Chase challenges. Teamwork—drifting, stunts, nitro boosts—and shared reputation points drive the core loop.
